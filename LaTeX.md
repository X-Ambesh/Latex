### Math mode in latex

```
A rectangle has side lengths of $(x+1)$ and $(x+3)$
the equation $A(x)=x^2+4x+3$
```
---

### To put the equation in same line use `${}$`

```tex
The equation ${A(x)=x^2+4x+3}$ gives the area of the rectangle.
```
---

### For display math mode use `$$ $$`

```tex
$${A(x)=x^2+4x+3}$$
```
